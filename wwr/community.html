What to expect

There are a lot of things that go into white water rafting. While it can be the most fun thing you've ever done, it also could be the last...!

Here are our top 10 tips for staying safe when rafting:

1.Choose a licensed and professional rafting outfitter.

2.Always wear a life jacket, or personal flotation device.

3.Be safe and comfortable. Wear the protective gear.

4.Make sure you have the right outerwear for the day.

5.Hold the paddle properly.

6.Stay in the boat!

7.Know the proper swimming techniques.

8.Know the commands - High-Siding.

9.Never panic!

10.Listen to your guide during the safety talk and on the water.
